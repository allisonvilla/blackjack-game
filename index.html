In âœ¨Almost Blackjackâœ¨ your goal is to draw cards until the total value of the cards in your hand reaches 21.

If you reach 21, you win! If you go over 21, you lose.

Betting

At the beginning of a round, you place a bet (minimum $5.00 to play). At the end of the round, your bet is deducted from your funds then awarded based on your win/lose conditions.

  • Reaching a sum of 21 wins you your full bet amount!
  • Reaching a sum of over 21 loses you your full bet amount.
  • Ending a round early (ie. before you reach or exceed a sum of 21) wins you a portion of your bet amount based on how close your current sum is to 21 (the closer you got, the more money you win back!).
  • Drawing a "natural" (ie. reaching 21 on your first draw) wins you twice your bet amount just for being lucky! ðŸ˜Š
